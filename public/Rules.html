This election is being conducted by the election commission of Nile university.

As a member of the constituency under the electoral districts of Nile university, you have the privilege to cast your vote. Follow these steps to ensure your vote is valid. It's crucial to make your decision independently, without any coercion or intimidation. If someone attempts to pressure or threaten you regarding your voting choice, please contact the provided helpline numbers immediately.

Steps:-

  • Have Security Keys with you as you would require it to proceed.
  • You are allowed to make only one vote per election.
  • You are allowed to choose only one candidate per election.
  • Candidates' information is available on the page.
  • Make sure that you are selecting your wanted candidate by confirming the name and symbol on the screen.
  • After selecting the candidate, make sure to submit the vote.
  • Results will be announced after 5 days of the election being completed.
  • You can verify your vote after the election results are announced.
